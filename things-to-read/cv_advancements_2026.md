# Computer Vision — Major Advancements in 2026

## Key Breakthroughs

- **YOLO26** — MuSGD optimizer (SGD + Muon hybrid from Kimi K2), NMS-free inference, 43% faster CPU, better small-object accuracy
- **Vision Transformers (ViTs)** — Now lead nearly every major CV benchmark, replacing CNNs for complex tasks
- **Foundation Models (SAM 2, DINOv2, DINOv3, CLIP)** — Zero/few-shot visual understanding at scale
- **Multimodal VLMs** — Vision + language + audio fusion is now standard; classic perception reframed as instruction-following and multi-step reasoning
- **3D Gaussian Splatting** — Khronos standardized it (glTF extension, Feb 2026); mainstream for robotics, AR/VR, SLAM
- **Synthetic Data & Self-Supervised Learning** — Generative models routinely create training data; digital twins simulate rare scenarios
- **Edge AI** — Real-time on-device vision for autonomous driving, industrial automation, surveillance
- **CV market hit ~$32B in 2026**
